Simple Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice is the ultimate family-friendly dish that yields maximum flavor without fuss. Loaded with tender sauteed veggies and delicious bits of scrambled egg, this top-rated recipe makes it easy to recreate a takeout favorite from home in just 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chicken
  • Seasonings
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Heat ann sprinkle cooking oil over pan
  2. Crack 3 eggs on pan
  3. Mix eggs
  4. Place bacon in pan mix it with the eggs
  5. Place Chicken in pan along with the rice
  6. Pour soy sauce into pan over all the ingredients
  7. Mix while it cooks until rice is a nice brown
  8. Enjoy!
